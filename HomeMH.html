A Message From The Creators of Happy Mind:

We, the creators of Happy Mind, are dedicated to spreading mental health awareness. On this website, we offer general information about a variety of mental illnesses; including tips and tricks, do's and dont's for helping your loved one's manage their illnesses, and perspectives from real people. We want people to find help wherever they are- which is why we implemented our tool, COBA; a map that assists in finding help near you. We hope that this website will help eradicate false notions surrounding mental illness, as well as be helpful for those seeking help for themselves. We want everyone to leave this site with the belief that they learned something new to apply to their lives.
